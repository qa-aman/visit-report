## How to Login

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Click on any persona card to login as that user
3. No password is required (demo mode)
4. You'll be redirected to the appropriate dashboard based on your role

## Testing Scenarios

### As Sales Engineer
1. Create a new visit report
2. Fill in all required fields
3. Add multiple contact persons
4. View your dashboard statistics
5. See your visit history

### As Team Leader
1. View team member visit reports
2. Check team statistics
3. Monitor individual team member performance
4. Review visit outcomes

### As Administrator
1. View system configuration
2. See predefined options
